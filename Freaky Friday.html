Rio de Janeiro

Rio de Janeiro is one of the biggest cities in Brazil and a popular destination for tourists. Rio de Janeiro is one of the most visited cities in South America and hosted the sommer olympics in 2016. With various famous landmarks like Christ the Redemer, Sugarloaf Mountain, Copacabana and the Maracana Stadium it is a beautiful country to visit. Rio was founded in 1565 by the portuguese and was a domain for the portuguese Empire. Rio is now the headquarters to Brazilian oil, mining and telecommunications companies. Brazil is also known for its Carnival, football and music like samba and bossa nova. Rio is a unique place worth traveling to in the post corona era.

Paris

Paris is the Capital of France and the most and has an estimated population of 2.148.271 residents as of 2020(wikipedia). Paris is one of the most visited cities in the world and has contributed greatly in the aspects of sports. Paris host the annual French Open Grand Slam tennis tournament and every July the Tour the France finishes in Paris. The city hosted the Olympic games in 1900,1924 and will host the Summer Olympics in 2024. Paris also have top culural attractions like Basilica of Sacre-Cour, Louvre and the famous Eiffel Tower. Since the 17th century Paris has been one of Europes major centres of finance, diplomacy, fashion, science and arts and is a fine citie to travel to.

Rome

Roma witch is the capital of Italy and the most populous city in Italy. Vatican City is an independent country inside the city boundries of Rome and the only existing country inside a city. Roman mythology dates the founding of Romw at around 753 BC but the area have been inhabited for much longer making it one of the oldest occupied cities in Europe.(wikipedia) Rome became the capital of the Roman Empire. Rome is one of the most important destination for tourists in the world. Tourists destination like the Musei Capitolini, Vatican Museum and the Catacombs along with the archeological and artistic treasures in the city makes it a perfect city for tourists. Rome also contains the Colosseum witch might be one of Rome's most iconic archeological sites and is regarded as a wonder of the world. Rome is definetly a place to visit as a tourist when the opportunity is there.

London

It is one of the most visited cities in Europe with a good reason and the same reasons should make you think about taking the trip.

New York

New York is the most populous city in the United States with an estimated population of 8,336,817 (wikipedia). New York is the most linguistically diverse city in the world and is home to the highest number of billionaires of any city in the world(wikipedia). New York also have many tourist destinations with Times Square, the Empire State building, the Statue of Liberty being one of the most popular. The city also have museums, parks and is a fine place for luxury shopping.
